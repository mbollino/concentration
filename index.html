Concentration

Can you beat the timer?

Instructions

Concentration is a game to test your memory! Can you match all the pairs before the timer runs out?

  1. Click "Start Game"
  2. The gameboard will appear and you will have 8 seconds to memorize the position of all the cards.
  3. Click your first card! This starts the countdown!
  4. Find the matching card before the timer runs out!!
  5. Find all the matches and clear the board to win!!

Give it a try! Do you dare??!!
