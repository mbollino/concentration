Concentration

Can you beat the timer?

Instructions

Concentration is a game to test your memory! Can you match all the pairs before the timer runs out?

  1. Click your first card! This will start the game timer.
  2. Find the matching card to clear the pair from the board.
  3. Find all the matches and clear the board before the timer runs out to win!!

Give it a try! Do you dare??!!
